About Municipalities Tourist Gallery

Manila is home to many famous tourist spots such as Rizal Park, Intramuros, the National Museum, Manila Ocean Park, Binondo, and Manila Cathedral, which together showcase the city’s rich history, culture, and entertainment. These destinations offer a mix of historical landmarks, museums, cultural heritage, and modern attractions, making Manila a popular place for tourists who want to explore the past and present of the Philippines in one city.
