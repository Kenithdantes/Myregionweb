Manila is home to many famous tourist spots such as Rizal Park, Intramuros, the National Museum, Manila Ocean Park, Binondo, and Manila Cathedral..The National Capital Region (NCR), also known as Metro Manila, is home to some of the Philippines’ most iconic tourist destinations. Visitors can explore Intramuros, the historic walled city that reflects Spanish-era heritage, relax at Rizal Park, and discover art and history at the National Museum Complex. Binondo Chinatown, the world’s oldest Chinatown, offers rich culture and famous local cuisine, while Manila Cathedral stands as a symbol of faith and history. Modern attractions like Manila Ocean Park add entertainment and family-friendly experiences, making NCR a perfect blend of history, culture, and modern city life.
