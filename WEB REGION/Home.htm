About NCR

The National Capital Region (NCR), also known as Metro Manila, is the political, economic, and cultural center of the Philippines. It is composed of 16 cities and 1 municipality, making it the most densely populated region in the country. NCR plays a major role in governance, business, education, and tourism.

History & Importance

NCR has a rich historical background, with Manila serving as the capital city of the Philippines. The region is home to important historical landmarks such as Intramuros, Rizal Park, and Malacañang Palace. Over the years, NCR has grown into a modern metropolitan area while preserving its cultural heritage.
